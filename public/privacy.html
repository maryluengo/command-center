Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026  ·  Command Center by María Luengo

This Privacy Policy describes how Command Center ("the App"), operated by María Luengo, handles information when you use it to connect your social media accounts and view your analytics.

1. Who This App Is For

Command Center is a personal productivity dashboard used exclusively by its owner, María Luengo. It is not a public service and does not have end-user sign-ups.

2. What Data Is Accessed

When you connect your Instagram account, the App reads the following data via the Instagram Graph API and Meta for Developers:

  • Instagram profile information (username, follower count, media count)
  • Post metrics (likes, comments, reach, impressions, saves)
  • Account-level insights (reach, impressions, profile views)

When you connect your TikTok account, the App reads the following via the TikTok API:

  • TikTok profile information (display name, follower count, likes)
  • Video metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)

3. How Data Is Stored

All analytics data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage or, in the desktop app, in memory. No analytics data is sent to any external server operated by this App.

OAuth access tokens are stored in a secure, HttpOnly cookie on the Vercel deployment, accessible only to the App's own serverless functions. They are never exposed to client-side JavaScript or logged.

4. Data Sharing

This App does not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. API calls are made directly to Meta / Instagram and TikTok from the App's backend. No analytics or social media data is stored in any shared or cloud database.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens stored in cookies expire automatically after 60 days. You can disconnect your accounts at any time by clicking "Disconnect" in the Analytics section, which immediately expires the cookie and clears cached data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party APIs. Please review their privacy policies for details on how they handle your data:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

As the sole user, you have full control over all data. You can disconnect accounts, clear localStorage, or delete cookies at any time to remove all stored information.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact hello@marialuengo.com.
